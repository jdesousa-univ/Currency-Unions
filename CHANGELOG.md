# Changelog

## 2026 version — 18 April 2026

### Coverage
- Extended from **1948–2023** (76 years, 17,860 observations) to **1948–2025** (78 years, 18,330 observations).
- Country count unchanged: **235 countries**.

### New multinational currency
- **Caribbean Guilder (XCG)** added as currency union **2b**, replacing the Netherlands Antillean guilder (ANG) in Curaçao and Sint Maarten from March 31, 2025.

### Corrections
- **Ecuador (ECU)**: USD start year corrected from `> 2000` to `>= 2000` (dollarization effective January 2000).
- **East Timor (TLS)**: USD from `>= 2000` added (UNTAET Regulation 2000/7 established USD as official currency in January 2000; maintained since independence in May 2002).
